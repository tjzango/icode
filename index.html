Our Mission

Empowering Future Innovators Through Coding

iCode Africa is a global social impact platform working to ensure that every K-12 student in every country around the world learns how to code, just like studying math, English, reading, and writing. Our vision is that every student with access to a computer or mobile device will get a chance to learn coding for free regardless of location or social limitations.
